How it Works

Once you have completed the Registration process, you will be engaged via a contract of employment which will then mean you are an employee of Extra Pay LTD, this means your tax and national insurance contributions will be made through PAYE. We will then establish a business to business contract with the agency/agencies or End client(s). whenever you work we will raise an invoice to the agency or end client(s).

The invoice amount will be paid to Extra Pay and we will then begin with the processing of your payroll. This will include deductions for our services (Administration fee) and Employers National insurance which will be paid to HMRC. The balance is your Salary which you will pay income tax and employee national insurance contributions on. The net amount will be transferred to your account and you will receive a payslip showing a detailed breakdown.
